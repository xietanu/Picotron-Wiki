# SFX Editor > Keybinds

## Overview

Information from here is sourced from the [Picotron Manual](https://www.lexaloffle.com/dl/docs/picotron_manual.html)

This goes over the keybinds for the Picotron SFX Editor.

## SFX and Pattern Editing modes

### Space
Pause/Resume playback

Use `CTRL+SPACE` to play from the cursor position. Use `SHIFT+SPACE` to play from the start of the current group of 8 rows.
